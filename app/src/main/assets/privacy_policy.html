BilaWoga Safety App - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

IMPORTANT: This app is designed for emergency situations. Your safety is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name: Your name for emergency identification
  • Emergency Contacts: Phone numbers of trusted contacts for emergency situations
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when SOS is activated

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Basic device details for app functionality
  • App Usage: SOS activation times and frequency (for abuse prevention)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Emergency Response: Send your location and emergency message to your contacts
  • App Functionality: Enable shake detection and SOS features
  • Security: Prevent app misuse and ensure legitimate emergency use

3. Data Security

Security Measures:
  • All personal data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Emergency messages are sent directly via SMS

4. Data Sharing

  • Emergency Contacts: Your location and emergency message are shared only with your designated emergency contacts
  • No Third Parties: We do not share your data with any third parties
  • No Analytics: We do not collect or share usage analytics

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all stored data within the app
  • Modify: You can update your name and emergency contacts anytime
  • Delete: You can clear all data using the logout feature
  • Control: You can disable location services or uninstall the app

6. Emergency Situations

Emergency Protocol:
  • When SOS is activated, your location and emergency message are sent to your contacts
  • This information is sent via SMS and is not stored by us
  • Your contacts receive only the information necessary for emergency response

7. Data Retention

  • Local Storage: Data is stored on your device until you clear it
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on external servers
  • Immediate Deletion: When you clear data, it is immediately and securely deleted

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app settings.

Emergency Disclaimer: This app is designed for emergency situations. In case of a real emergency, always contact local emergency services (911, 112, etc.) immediately.
